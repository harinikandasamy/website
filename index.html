About Us

Machine hole scavenging bot aims at eradicating manual scavenging,thus making manhole to machine hole and thus implements safai mitra suraksha challenge.

Abstract

Manual Scavenging, an illegal outlawed activity, is still happening all over the world despite laws, this has led to the death of lakhs of people.Scavenging is not properly mechanized yet in India, though machines like Bandicoot are existing. ​

The main reason that could be considered for the non-implementation of existing machine scavenging methods can be said as cost primarily.​According to the Safaimitra Suraksha Challenge of India, Nov 2020, India aims at cleaning manholes with machines in 243 cities before August 2021.

Our goal here primarily is to develop a Machine Hole Scavenging Bot, that could clean manholes by taking out solid wastes that block the liquid flow through the sewage system, thus not making the scavengers lose their job but upgrade their standard from manual scavengers to machine operators

Solution, We are Working On

  • 01Standing and Drone unit

    Drone unit is the one which gets inside the manhole,inspects and picks the wastes out. The standing unit,provides enough support to the drone unit from the roads over the manhole.

  • 02Jack Unit

    The drone unit will be lowered into the manhole using a cable which can't provide the expected stability, there will be oscillations.Hence the Jack unit aids the drone unit by fixing themselves to the inner walls of manhole.

  • 03 Hygeine and sanitation

    Specially designed Clamshell Bucket endeffector enters and removes the solid waste and blocks from the manhole thus preventing clogging and flooding on roads.

  • 04Locating Blocks/Solid Wastes Easily

    The 6 DOF parallel manipulator and camera-sensor system helps in locating the wastes and aids the end effector collect it.

Features

  • Water Resistant

    Coatings and the materials choosen will be able to support the bot in adverse situations of manhole.

  • Semi Automatic

    Reduces the risks to manual scavengers without making them losing their jobs.

  • Human Safety

    As our Bot aims at no human intervention inside the manhole,human safety is assured.

  • Cost efficient

    Our mechanisms makes it affordable for the government

  • Machine Vision

    Cameras and sensors fixed at the endeffector helps in finding out the blocks inside the manhole easily

  • User Friendly

    The display panel helps the operator to have a vision of manhole environment, and the control panel will be in such a way that assists even illiterated to work with.
